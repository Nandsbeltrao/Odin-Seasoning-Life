Ingredients

  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice (approximately 4-6 lemons, depending on size and juiciness)
  • 1 cup of sugar (or to taste, adjust according to preference)
  • 5 cups of cold water
  • Ice to taste
  • Lemon wedges for garnish (optional)
  • Basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Lemon Juice: Squeeze the lemons to get 1 cup of fresh juice. If you don't have a juicer, you can use a fork to extract the juice, but a juicer makes the process easier and helps extract more juice.
  2. Make the Simple Syrup: In a small pot, mix 1 cup of sugar with 1 cup of water. Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is completely dissolved. This creates a simple syrup that mixes better with the lemonade. After the sugar is dissolved, remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Mix the Lemonade: In a large pitcher, combine the lemon juice, cooled simple syrup, and 4 cups of cold water. Stir well until everything is well mixed.
  4. Adjust the Flavor: Taste the lemonade and adjust the flavor as necessary. If it's too sweet, add more water. If you prefer it sweeter, add a little more sugar or syrup.
  5. Serve: Add ice to the pitcher or directly to the glasses before serving. Garnish with lemon wedges and basil leaves, if desired.
  6. Store: If there's leftover lemonade, you can store it in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Always stir well before serving again.

Tips ;)

  • For a special touch, you can add a bit of grated ginger or a handful of fruits like strawberries or raspberries to infuse a different flavor.
