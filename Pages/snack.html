Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or 1 Italian bread (or any other bread of your choice)
  • 100 g butter (at room temperature)
  • 4 garlic cloves (chopped or minced)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Garlic Butter: In a medium bowl, mix the softened butter with the chopped garlic. If using Parmesan cheese, add it to the mixture as well. Stir until everything is well combined. Add the parsley, salt, black pepper, and oregano (if using). Mix until the mixture is smooth.
  2. Prepare the Bread: Preheat the oven to 180¬∞C (350¬∞F). Cut the baguette or Italian bread in half lengthwise to form two halves. If the bread is very large, you can cut it into smaller portions.
  3. Spread the Butter: Using a spatula or knife, generously spread the garlic butter mixture over the cut sides of the bread.
  4. Bake the Bread: Place the bread halves on a baking sheet. Put in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the butter is melted and the bread is golden and crispy.
  5. Finish: If you want extra crispiness, you can put the bread under the broiler for 1-2 minutes, but keep an eye on it to avoid burning.
  6. Serve: Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool slightly before cutting into pieces and serving.

Tips ;)

  • For an extra touch of flavor, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or paprika to the butter.
