Ingredients

  • 500 g of ground beef (chuck or round steak works well)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil or olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped"
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced or mashed
  • 1 green or red bell pepper, chopped (optional)
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (or smoked paprika)
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder (adjust according to taste for spiciness)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of beef broth or water
  • 1/2 cup of cooked black or red beans (optional, for a more robust version)
  • Corn or wheat tortillas (preferably warmed before serving)
  • Shredded cheese (like cheddar or mozzarella)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomato
  • Red onion slices
  • Chopped fresh coriander (optional)
  • Avocado slices or guacamole
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat: Heat the oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion and sautÃ© until it becomes translucent. Add the garlic and bell pepper (if using) and sautÃ© for another 1-2 minutes. Add the ground beef and cook until it is well browned, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon or spatula as it cooks. If using tomatoes, add them along with the meat and cook until the tomatoes start to break down.
  2. Season the Meat: Add cumin, paprika, chili powder, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine. Add the beef broth or water and stir. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, until the meat is well-seasoned and the liquid has reduced somewhat. If using beans, add them now and mix well.
  3. Assemble the Tacos: Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or in the oven for a few minutes, if preferred. Place the seasoned meat in a tortilla. Add shredded cheese, lettuce, tomato, red onion, and cilantro, if using. Top with avocado slices, guacamole, sour cream, and hot sauce, if desired.
  4. Serve: Serve the tacos immediately while they are still warm and crispy.

Tips ;)

  • For a nicer presentation, arrange the tacos on a platter and let everyone build their own tacos with their preferred fillings.
