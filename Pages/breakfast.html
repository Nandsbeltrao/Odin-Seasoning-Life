Ingredients

  • Fresh or Frozen Fruits: 1 ripe banana 1 cup of strawberries (fresh or frozen) 1/2 cup of blueberries (fresh or frozen) 1/2 apple (optional, chopped into pieces)
  • Liquid: 1 cup of milk (can be cow's milk, almond milk, soy milk, or other plant-based milk) 1/2 cup of orange juice (optional, for an extra touch of freshness)
  • Sweetener (optional): 1-2 tablespoons of honey, agave syrup, or another sweetener of your choice
  • Other Ingredients (optional): 1 tablespoon of chia seeds or flaxseeds for a fiber boost 1/2 cup of plain or Greek yogurt for a creamier smoothie Ice to taste, if using fresh fruits

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fruits: If using fresh fruits, wash them thoroughly and, if necessary, peel and cut them into smaller pieces. If using frozen fruits, you can skip the ice step, as the frozen fruits will already provide a creamier texture.
  2. Blend the Ingredients: Add all the ingredients to the blender: the fruits, milk, and orange juice (if using). If you choose to use a sweetener, add it at this stage as well.
  3. Blend Until Smooth: Blend everything on high speed until the mixture is completely smooth and creamy. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add a bit more milk to reach the desired consistency.
  4. Add Extra Ingredients: If you're using chia seeds, flaxseeds, or yogurt, add them now and blend a little longer to combine everything thoroughly.
  5. Taste and Adjust: - Taste the smoothie and adjust the flavor if needed. Add more sweetener if desired, or a bit more fruit if you think it needs more flavor.
  6. Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and, if desired, garnish with fresh fruit pieces or a sprinkle of granola on top.
  7. Enjoy! Savor your refreshing and healthy smoothie!

Tips ;)

  • If you're short on time, use frozen fruits for a quicker preparation.
